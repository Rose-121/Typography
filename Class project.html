The Art of Drawing Time 🖌️>>>


After melodical parts i've to take place of Painting. Art is at the heart of everything I do. It’s not just something I enjoy it’s how I express myself. I spend my free time drawing, always making space for creativity in my life. I find joy in creating traditional landscapes, bringing animes to life, and pouring my passion into calligraphy . Over the years, my calligraphy has been featured in >Art Exhibitions in 2023, 2024, and 2025, with some pieces even winning prestigious awards. Just like my love for singing, drawing has been a source of countless awards and recognition, but more than that, it’s the deep connection I have with art that truly makes it special. Drawing is my passion and my forever journey. 🎨✨

Writing Beyond Imagination ✍️🚀>>>


Whenever I get the chance, I immerse myself in writing science fiction. It’s a passion that allows me to channel all the fascinating thoughts and ideas that come to my mind. I find that I can express myself far better through writing than speaking, as it gives me the freedom to explore endless possibilities. Some of my best work includes two short stories, 'Ava' and 'Oporotro Prithibi,' which were honored to be included in Faridpur’s DC Sir's book. It’s a surreal feeling to see my words make their way into such a prestigious compilation. Beyond that, I also teamed up with my friends to create a book filled with science fiction stories, all written by us. This collaboration was a dream come true and a testament to our shared passion for creativity and storytelling. 📚

In Literature 📖💭🫶🏻>>>


Literature holds a special place in my heart, and reading is one of my greatest passions. Novels, in particular, captivate me, and I find myself completely absorbed in the worlds they create. Some of my favorite writers include Zafar Iqbal, Arif Azad, Kazi Nazrul Islam, and Zahir Raihan, whose words have had a lasting impact on me. While I may not have as much time to read fiction at the moment, it’s something I deeply miss and cherish. My love for literature goes beyond just reading—I also have a passion for collecting books, always looking for new additions to my collection. Books, for me, are not just stories, they’re windows to endless worlds, ideas, and adventures.📖
